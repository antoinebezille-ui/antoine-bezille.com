02 — Projects

Digital Assets & Tokenisation

Real-World Asset Tokenisation

stran.ch
RWA Token Platform

↗ stran.ch

I am deeply interested in tokenisation — the process of bringing real-world assets on-chain — and in the crypto ecosystem at large. I create tokens and NFTs, and I am co-founding stran.ch, an early-stage startup (access code: fidelio) working toward real-world asset tokenisation focused on Swiss real estate.

We are currently in the building phase — no product is live yet. Our aim is to enable fractional ownership of Swiss real estate through on-chain tokens, making an asset class historically inaccessible to retail investors available from CHF 100. The planned infrastructure runs on Polygon, with smart contracts designed to handle yield distribution, compliance, and DEX trading.

Blockchain

Polygon — EVM-compatible, low-fee, high-throughput infrastructure planned for token deployment and smart contract execution.

Token & NFT Creation

I design and deploy fungible tokens and NFTs — from tokenised asset shares to digital collectibles and access-gated assets.

Smart Contracts

Solidity contracts in development covering ERC-3643 compliance, automated yield distribution, multisig governance, and upgradeable proxies.

Vision

Our goal is to build a FINMA-aware tokenisation framework under Swiss DLT-Lex 2021, making real estate investable for retail from CHF 100.
